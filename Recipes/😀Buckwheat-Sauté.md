## Buckwheat Sauté

Ingredients:

* 4 oz cabbage
* ¼ cup (~1 oz cooked) dried buckwheat
* 1.8 oz onion
* 0.5 oz olive oil (1 tbsp)
* 3 oz carrots
* 4 oz tomato
* 3 oz spinach
* ¼ tsp sea salt
* ¼ tsp pepper

---

## Macros:

* Protein: ~6g
* Fat: ~15g
* Net Carbs: ~21.5g
* Fiber: ~8.5g
* Calories: ~270 kcal
* Sodium: ~600mg
* Potassium: ~750mg
